--height or --aspect – Adjust the row scaling.
--brightness and --contrast – Adjust image intensity.
--grayscale {average|luminosity|custom} – Different grayscale formulas.
--custom-ramp – Let the user supply a custom ASCII ramp string.
DONE --invert – Invert the intensity mapping.
DONE --flip-x / --flip-y – Flip horizontally or vertically.
--dither – Enable or choose a dithering algorithm.
--color – Output approximate color in ASCII using ANSI escape codes.
